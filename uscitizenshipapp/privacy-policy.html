Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the US Citizenship Interview Prep app ("App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our application.

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

2. Data Stored Locally

All study progress, quiz results, and bookmarks are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking technologies.

4. No Internet Connection Required

The App functions entirely offline. No data is sent to external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, the App is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the App Store support link on our app listing.
